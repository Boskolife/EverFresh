Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

EverFresh Paintworks (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website or submit a quote or contact request.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly, including:

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Property type (e.g. home or business)
  • Painting scope (exterior, interior, or both)
  • Preferred days and times for contact or scheduling
  • Any other details you include in your message or form submission

We may also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our Site (e.g. IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited). This may be used for analytics, security, and improving the Site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide quotes or estimates
  • Schedule and manage appointments or projects
  • Send you updates about your request or our services (by phone, email, or text, as you have agreed)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only:

  • With service providers that help us operate the Site or communicate with you (e.g. email delivery or form-handling services), subject to confidentiality and data protection obligations
  • When required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property
  • With your consent or as otherwise described at the time of collection

Form submissions may be processed via third-party services (e.g. EmailJS). Their use of data is governed by their own privacy policies; we encourage you to review them.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our Site may use cookies or similar technologies for functionality, analytics, or preferences. You can adjust your browser settings to limit or block cookies; some features of the Site may not work as intended if you do so.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy (e.g. to complete your project, respond to inquiries, and comply with legal requirements). You may ask us to delete or correct your data subject to applicable law.

6. Your Rights (Canada / Ontario)

Under applicable Canadian privacy law (including PIPEDA where it applies), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Withdraw consent where we rely on consent for processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a privacy regulator

To exercise these rights or ask questions about our practices, please contact us using the contact options on the Site.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

9. Children

Our Site is not directed at minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when we make material changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact Us

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through the quote form or other contact details provided on the EverFresh Paintworks website.
